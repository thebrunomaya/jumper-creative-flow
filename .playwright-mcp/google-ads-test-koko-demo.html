WHAT WORKED ‚úÖ

Success Factors

1. QUIZ AS ENTRY POINT

  • Psychological "safe space" for symptom assessment
  • 8.2:1 conversion advantage over direct CTA
  • Essential for sensitive health topics
  • Creates ownership of health decision

2. DISPLAY EFFICIENCY

  • Ultra-low CPCs: $0.01-0.03 average
  • 56% of total conversion volume
  • Scales without efficiency loss
  • Receptive audience to visual + quiz

3. ALGORITHM MATURITY

  • Week 1-2: Exploration phase
  • Week 3-4: Optimization gains (-35-37% weekly)
  • Week 5: Mature scaling (2x budget absorbed)
  • CPC optimization: $0.08 ‚Üí $0.028

4. GEOGRAPHIC DIVERSIFICATION

  • Reduced single-market risk (74% ‚Üí 23%)
  • Colombia & Panama emerged as scale leaders
  • 6-market portfolio proved manageable

WHAT DIDN'T WORK ‚ö†Ô∏è

Constraints Identified

1. EH CAMPAIGNS - STRUCTURAL FAILURE

  • Display EH: 0 conversions in 5 of 6 countries
  • Search EH: $0.88-4.22/conversion (prohibitive)
  • Diagnosis: Topic too sensitive without quiz buffer
  • Total waste: ~$180 for 79 conversions

2. POLICY RESTRICTIONS - SCALE CEILING

  • 15 of 24 campaigns "limited by policy"
  • Artificial delivery cap at ~$400/week
  • Root cause: Medical terminology + symptom focus
  • Impact: Cannot scale beyond current level
  • Status: Unresolved throughout 5 weeks

  • Honduras Search: $0.41 ‚Üí $0.88 (+114% in 1 week)
